üé¨ Getting Started

Welcome to Bollywood Cinema! You're now a movie producer. Your goal is to create successful films and build your reputation in the industry.

üìù Production Pipeline

Every project follows these stages:

  • Script: Create your project and hire a writer
  • Crew: Assemble your cast and music team
  • Production: Film your project (12-16 weeks)
  • Marketing: Promote your project to audiences
  • Distribution: Choose release strategy
  • Release: Pick the perfect date and see results

üí° Success Tips

  • Match your crew's specialties with your project's genre
  • Avoid releasing during heavy competition
  • Invest in marketing for better awareness
  • Festival dates can boost earnings but increase competition
  • Build franchises from successful movies

üìä Managing Resources

Keep track of your budget and reputation. Successful projects increase both, while failures can hurt your standing in the industry.
